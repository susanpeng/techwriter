11.2. Introduction

The Bookshelf Editor is software for storing and organizing URLs. It is provided by Online-learning.com for the course assignments completed during the Professional Technical Writing Course. The tested version of the Bookshelf Editor application is 1.1.

The objectives of the test are to investigate the following:

  • The information presented in the Bookshelf Editor manual is complete and accurate
  • The manual is helpful to end users in accomplishing the basic tasks
  • The answers to the specific questions are easy to find
  • The language used in the manual is clear and concise
  • The manual instructions contain sufficient amount of detail to successfully complete a specific task
  • The reference information for commands, controls, options is clear and complete
