5.4. Changing the alignment of a shelf

To change the alignment of the shelf:

  1. Click the shelf you want to change.

    The red arrow indicates the currently selected shelf.

  2. Do one of the following:
    • From the Tool Panel, click Shelf Properties.
    • From the Bookshelf menu, click Shelf Properties.
  3. In the Shelf Properties dialog box, click Left, Center, or Right under Justification, and then click OK.

    The alignment of the shelf is changed as specified.
