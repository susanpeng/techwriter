6.4. Adding a title to a book

To add a title to a book:

  1. Select a book.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • In the Tool Panel, click Book Properties.
    • On the Object menu, click Book Properties.
  3. In the Object Properties dialog box, in the Title field, enter the title for your book.

    The title you entered appears on the spine of your book.
