1.1. What is the Bookshelf Editor

The Bookshelf Editor 1.1 is a compact application designed to help you store, organize and manage links to online content.

The user interface has a familiar look of “shelves” filled with “books” representing the URL links to your favorite websites. Locating and accessing your favorite website are as easy and convenient as picking a book from a shelf. All you need to do is to browse the book titles on your virtual shelves and click the book(s) you want to "read".

The Bookshelf Editor provides you with a variety of convenient built-in tools that help you to create your own design and arrangement of shelves and books. The Bookshelf Editor enables you to organize your books in a manner that makes sense to you. Using the Bookshelf Editor, you can:

  • Create multiple shelves of various sizes
  • Group books on related topics by placing them on different shelves
  • Change the presentation on each shelf by adding spaces between books
  • Add titles to your books
  • Change book appearance

The Bookshelf Editor also enables you to export your project in XML or HTML. Once you have exported your bookshelf, you can view it on any computer; you can even email it to a friend.

You can create and export as many bookshelves as you want and dedicate each to a separate project or topic. Also, you can carry your collection in a flash drive and access it from any web-enabled computer.
