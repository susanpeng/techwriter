1.3. How to use the manual

This User's Manual contains the detailed information you need to know to use Bookshelf Editor.

The manual is written with the assumption that you are familiar with browsing the Internet and performing basic Windows functions.

A mini flowchart with a right angle bracket, such as File > Save, indicates a series of menu choices.

You can navigate through the manual using the hyperlinks and buttons in the following standard aids and features:

  • Table of Contents

    Presents a hierarchical list of sections and topics and links them to their content.

  • Navigation arrows

    Enable you to move one page back or forward.

  • Browser navigation buttons

    Enable you to move one screen back or forward in your session history.

  • Breadcrumbs

    Show your location in the manual hierarchy and provide hyperlinks to higher-level sections.

  • Index

    Maps the specific words, expressions, and terms used in the manual to the pages where you can find related material.
