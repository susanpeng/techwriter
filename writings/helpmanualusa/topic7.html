2.1. Downloading the software

To download Bookshelf Editor 1.1:

  1. On web page http://courses.online-learning.com, log in to your course page.
  2. Under Navigation, click My course.
  3. Click the name of your course.
  4. In the course information page, click the Course downloads link.
  5. On the Course downloads page, click BookshelfEditor11.exe.

    The application file BookshelfEditor11.exe is downloaded to your Downloads directory.
