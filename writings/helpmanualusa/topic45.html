6.3. Changing the color of a book

To change a color of a book:

  1. Select the book you want to change.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • In the Tool Panel, click Book Properties.
    • On the Object menu, click Book Properties.
  3. In the Object Properties dialog box, from the Colour list, click Gray-Green, Blue-Gray, Mauve, or Sky Blue, and then click OK.

    The color of your book has changed to the selected color.
