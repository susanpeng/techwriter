4.3.2. Viewing the Construction

To view the construction of the bookshelf:

  1. Do one of the following:
    • From the Tool Panel, click Design Properties.
    • From the Bookshelf menu, click Design Properties.
  2. In the Design Properties dialog box, view the Construction of the bookshelf.
