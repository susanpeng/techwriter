4.3.1. Adding a Bookshelf Title

To add a bookshelf title, do one of the following:

  • In the Bookshelf group box in the Tool Panel, type the bookshelf title in the Title text box.

Or

  1. Select Bookshelf > Design Properties.
  2. In the Bookshelf Title box, type the bookshelf title.
