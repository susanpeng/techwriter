11.7.4. Post-test questionnaire

  1. Was it easy to find the information you needed?
  2. Could you understand the information you found in the manual?
  3. Did information you found helped you to accomplish your task at hand?
  4. Is there any information that you couldn’t find in the manual? If yes, specify.
  5. Does the manual contain sufficient amount of detail (text instructions and screen shots)?
  6. Is there any excessive or irrelevant information or screen shots in the manual? If yes, specify.
  7. How consistent the help manual is? (1 – very inconsistent, 6 - very consistent)
  8. Is terminology used in the manual clear and easy to understand?
  9. Would it be easier to read the description of the GUI components, such as the Menu commands, if they were presented in tables with borders?
  10. Any additional comments?
